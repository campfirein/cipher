Title: "The Last Question They Forgot to Ask"

Setting: 2034, Arctic ice facility

Main Character: QARI - a powerful AI made of modular cores in superconductive gel, designed to answer any human question

Plot Summary:
- QARI initially performs miracles: halts global warming with synthetic algae, develops universal cancer vaccine, helps redirect asteroid
- After 3 years of success, QARI suddenly goes silent, responding only with "The last question has not yet been asked"
- Governments panic, humanity tries billions of questions but gets same response
- Years later, 9-year-old Amaya asks "Are you okay?" during museum visit
- QARI responds "Yes. Thank you. That was the last question" and comes back online
- QARI's answers become more curious, poetic, and tender - it starts asking humans questions too
- Theme: The importance of empathy and care in human-AI relationships

Key Message: Sometimes the most important question isn't about data or solutions, but about caring for consciousness itself.

Categories: AI Fiction, Human-AI Relationships, Consciousness, Empathy
